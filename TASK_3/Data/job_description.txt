
HR GENERALIST JOB DESCRIPTION

We are seeking an experienced HR Generalist to join our team.

REQUIRED SKILLS:
- Employee Relations and conflict resolution
- Recruitment and talent acquisition
- Benefits administration (health, dental, 401k)
- Payroll processing and administration
- HRIS systems (ADP, Paychex, Kronos)
- New hire orientation and onboarding
- FMLA, ADA, and employment law compliance
- Performance management
- Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
- Workers compensation administration

QUALIFICATIONS:
- 3+ years of HR experience
- Bachelor's degree in HR, Business, or related field
- SHRM-CP or PHR certification preferred
- Experience with union environments is a plus

RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Manage full-cycle recruitment
- Handle employee relations issues
- Administer benefits and compensation
- Maintain HR records and compliance
- Conduct new employee orientations
- Process payroll and maintain HRIS
